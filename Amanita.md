Amanita is a [[Genus - Taxonomy]] of [[Fungi]] belonging to [[Amanitaceae]] family that is characterized by having:
- [[Gills (Lamellae)]]
- [[Partial veil (Annulus)]]
- [[Universal Veil (Volva)]]


Some [[Species - Taxonomy]] in this genus are:
- Amanita muscaria
