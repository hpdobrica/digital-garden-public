The ring located below the [[Cap (Pileus)]] and [[Gills (Lamellae)]] of a [[Mushroom]] is called **Annulus (partial veil)**. It's used to cover the [[Hymenium]] until the fruiting body matures.

See [[Morphology of a Mushroom]]
