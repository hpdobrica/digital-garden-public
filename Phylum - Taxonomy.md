Phylum is the rank in [[Taxonomy of Living Organisms]], that comes after [[Kingdom - Taxonomy]] and before [[Class - Taxonomy]].

Each kingdom contains a number of phyla:
- Animalia: ~31
- Plantae: ~14
- [[Fungi]]: ~8 (see [[Fungi phyla]])
