Agaricales is the name of [[Fungi order]] belonging to [[Basidiomycota]] [[Fungi phylum]] that are characterized by having [[Gills (Lamellae)]].

It contains 46 families that aren't extinct.

Some families in this order are:
- [[Amanitaceae]]
